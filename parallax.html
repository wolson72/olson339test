Artists I'm Into

Overall, I would call myself first a fan of the Grateful Dead (nearly all eras). However, stopping there I would be doing a disservice to all the other music I love. Some other artists include Duster, Weezer, and Barrignton Levy. There are many more genres and artists but as it is with most people, it all depends on the mood!

Favorite Albums

Below are some of my most-listened to albums over these past couple of weeks. They truly run the gammut across many moods, styles, and eras. However, this is not a definitive list and will most likely have changed once the week is done!

This particular grateful dead album is seen as a classic by many fans. Originally performed in 1976, this show features a performance of St. Stephen, a song not usually performed after their early years in the 1960s.

Stratosphere by Duster was originally released in 1998 as their debut album. The sound on this album could be most readily described as "lo-fi", making it perfect for a rainy day inside.

Neutral Milk Hotel's 1998 album, In the Aeroplane Over the Sea is considered a cult classic by many. With outside the box lyrics and hectic imnstrumentals there, is undoubtley psychadelic quality to the songs here.
